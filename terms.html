1. About Us

Bunyaan SCIO (Scottish charity number SC055377) is Scotland's first robotics team for Muslim youth, delivering robotics and STEM education to young people aged 6 to 18 in Edinburgh. Bunyaan is open to all, regardless of background.

Contact: bootcamp@bunyaan.org.uk (bookings and events) or info@bunyaan.org.uk (general enquiries).

2. Bookings and Payment

  • All bookings are made and paid for online through our payment provider, Stripe.
  • A place is only confirmed once payment has been received in full.
  • Prices are as shown at the time of booking. We reserve the right to change prices for future bookings, but this will never affect a booking you have already confirmed and paid for.
  • You must provide accurate information about the participant, including age, at the time of booking, so we can place them in the right group.

3. If You Need to Cancel or Change a Booking

We understand that plans change, so please let us know as early as possible if you need to cancel your child's place.

  • More than 7 days before the session: we will offer to transfer the booking to another upcoming Bunyaan session, or refund the amount paid, minus any costs we have already committed on your child's behalf (for example, materials or a kit purchased specifically for them). We will always explain any costs deducted.
  • Within 7 days of the session: we are unable to offer a refund. By this point we have committed to costs for your child's place and are very unlikely to be able to fill it from a waitlist. At our discretion, we may still be able to offer a transfer to a future session instead.
  • Refunds are returned to your original payment method and, per Stripe's standard processing times, may take 5 to 10 business days to appear on your statement.

4. If We Need to Cancel or Change a Session

If Bunyaan cancels a session, or makes a material change to the date, time or venue, you will be offered a full refund or a transfer to an alternative session, in line with your rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015.

5. Health, Safety, Allergies and Medical Information

  • Before each session, we ask for information about any allergies, medical conditions or additional support needs, so we can look after your child properly.
  • Please make sure this information is accurate and up to date. Let us know immediately if anything changes before the session.
  • A first-aid trained adult will be present at all sessions.

6. Code of Conduct

We expect all participants to treat staff, volunteers and other children with respect. In the rare case of a serious behavioural or safety concern, we may need to contact a parent to collect a child early. We will always try to work with families first before this becomes necessary.

7. Safeguarding

Bunyaan takes the safety of young people seriously. All staff and volunteers working directly with children are registered under Scotland's PVG (Protecting Vulnerable Groups) scheme. Our Designated Safeguarding Lead can be contacted at info@bunyaan.org.uk for any safeguarding concern.

8. Photography and Video

We may take photographs or video during sessions for use on our website, social media, or in reports to funders. We will always ask for your separate, specific consent for this as part of your booking, and you are free to decline without affecting your child's place. You can withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us.

9. Data Protection

We collect and use personal information (including your contact details and any health or medical information you provide) in accordance with UK GDPR and our Privacy Policy. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
