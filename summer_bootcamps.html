The Bootcamp

Bunyaan's Summer Robotics Bootcamp brings young people face to face with engineers and academics for two days of hands-on robotics. Build a real robot, program it, compete as a team, and present your work.

What You Will Do

What happens over the two days.

Build It

Assemble a real robot from components. No experience needed.

Code It

Block coding and Python. Make your robot move, sense, and respond.

Challenge It

Team competitions on the day. Strategy, speed, and collaboration.

Present It

Explain your build to the group. Communicate like an engineer.

What You Leave With

Skills that last beyond the weekend.

Hands-on robotics and electronics skills
Block coding and Python programming
Engineering problem-solving under pressure
Communicating technical work to an audience
